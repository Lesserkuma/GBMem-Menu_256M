GBMem-Menu 256M Builder

Version 1.0

This tool builds a custom compilation of games for supported cartridges by modifying a user-provided NP GB-Memory Cartridge Menu ROM.

Supported cartridges are known as 256M Multi Game Cartridges and are available on marketplaces under generic names such as “108 in 1”.
